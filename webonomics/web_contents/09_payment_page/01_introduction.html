8.1. Payment Page¶

In this section we will create the Payment Page for our E-Commerce Website - MyZone.in

8.1.1. Requirement¶

Let us have a look on the basic requirement of this page :

  1. A background color.
  2. A heading and the Product Name, Price and Quantity.
  3. Payment Options, in which COD is enabled.

#. Form fields for Name, Contact Number, Email, Quantity, Total(which will be calculated itself) and the Delivery address.

  1. Number field should have proper valdation.
  2. Quantity should be less than available in stock.
